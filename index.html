Welcome to AANGAN

Where Tradition Meets Taste and Every Meal Feels Like Home

At AANGAN, we believe that food is more than just a meal—it is a way of bringing people together. Inspired by the warmth of a traditional Indian courtyard, AANGAN offers an authentic dining experience rooted in Maharashtrian culture and hospitality. Every dish is prepared using carefully selected ingredients, traditional recipes, and a passion for preserving the rich flavours of our heritage. Whether it is a family celebration, a gathering with friends, or a quiet meal with loved ones, AANGAN creates moments that feel familiar, comforting, and memorable. From our signature Maharashtrian specialties to timeless Indian favourites, every plate is served with care, affection, and a commitment to quality. Come, take a seat in our Aangan, and experience the taste of tradition.
